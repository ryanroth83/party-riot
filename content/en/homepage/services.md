### Balloon Art

Whether it is a birthday party, a neighborhood block party, or a school event, I enjoy taking the time to make each animal or shape special.

- **Birthday Parties:** Custom hats, animals, and crowns.
- **Community Events:** Entertainment for local gatherings.
- **Special Requests:** Feel free to ask for something unique!

---

### Pricing & Booking

To keep the business side simple and professional, I use a flat-rate approach for all events:

**Rate:** $20 per hour  
**Minimum:** 2 hours per booking  

This covers my time, all balloon materials, and my setup. Since I am a homeschool student, I often have a flexible schedule for weekday events or community visits.

---

### Logistics & Safety

Because I am a student, this is a family-supported venture. While I handle the creative work, my parents manage the "behind-the-scenes" details. 

All bookings, scheduling, and final logistics are reviewed by my parents, and they always accompany me to every event to ensure a safe and professional experience for everyone.

---

### Community & Volunteering

One of the reasons I started **Party Riot** was to share a little joy with others. I am especially passionate about using my skills to help kids who might be going through a difficult time.

If you are organizing a charitable event for a **hospital, shelter, or local non-profit**, please reach out. I would love to discuss volunteering my time to twist balloons for your cause. We always try our best to make time for organizations making a difference in the Houston area.
